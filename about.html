About

Hello,

My name is Md Shadman Tajwar Haque. I am from Bangladesh. I graduated in the year 2018 from American International University - Bangladesh (AIUB) with a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering.

Currently, an International Student working on my Masters of Engineering degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Windsor, ON. After completing my Bachelors of Science degree, I did an internship at a leading telecommunications company in Bangladesh where I worked as a Service Assurance and Experience Technology Intern.
